Week-by-Week Curriculum

Week 1 — Basics & Sensors

Deliverable: Simple sensor circuit that reports to serial/phone

  • Intro to microcontrollers (micro:bit / Arduino Nano RP2040 / CircuitPython on Feather)
  • Wiring, LEDs, sensors (distance / light), basic motor control
  • Safety protocols and proper handling of electronics

Week 2 — Movement & Control

Deliverable: Robot that can drive and stop on sensor input

  • Motor drivers, PWM, power safety
  • Chassis assembly and mechanical design
  • Build the robot body and get basic movement working (forward/back/turn)

Week 3 — Behavior, Deploy & Demo

Deliverable: Demo-ready robot + short case-study

  • Add logic (line-following, obstacle avoidance) or remote control (Bluetooth / simple web dashboard)
  • Safety, battery management, packaging for demo day
  • Practice pitch and record demo video
  • Demo day presentation to parents and school
