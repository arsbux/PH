Frequently Asked Questions

Do students need prior coding experience?

No. We accept complete beginners and those with experience. Projects are scaffolded by skill level.

What technology do you teach?

Frontend basics (HTML/CSS/JavaScript or block-based tools for younger kids), simple backend or static deploy workflows, and one practical AI or automation feature per project. Advanced ML/data tracks are coming soon.

What if my child can't finish the project?

We structure cohorts in teams and pairs so deliverables are achievable. If a student doesn't complete the project due to excused absence, we offer a make-up path.

Is this safe for kids?

Yes. Parental consent, media consent, instructor vetting, and a child-safety policy are required for every participant.
