Week-by-Week Curriculum

Week 1 — Problem & Customer (Discovery)

Deliverable: Validated problem statement + lean test plan

  • Teach rapid problem validation: 5-minute interviews
  • One-page problem statement
  • Target user persona development
  • Market research and validation techniques

Week 2 — Build & Launch Page

Deliverable: Live landing page with clear CTA

  • Rapid MVP or landing page (could be from Vibe graduates)
  • Messaging, pricing options, and basic funnel
  • Pre-order / sign-up / paid pilot setup
  • Conversion optimization basics

Week 3 — Distribution & Test

Deliverable: Evidence of at least one user interaction

  • Outreach scripts and targeted outreach strategies
  • WhatsApp groups, local businesses, school networks
  • Run the first test (pre-sale or validated sign-ups)
  • Demo day: present results and next steps
